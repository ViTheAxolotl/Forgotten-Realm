Map of Kasie's Islands

Kasie's Islands are is independent. However, with their proximity to Skyward, Skyward is involved with their lives. Skyward provided guards and other products, but the islands are considered their own country.

Fanup

Fanup is the biggest island of Kasie's Islands. Fanup is a fishing island, its society is based on if you can use a rod. Those who can't fish teach, or build the boats. They also herd animals. Making this island the best meat producer for the islands. 70% of what they kill and catch goes to Skyward.

Youb

Youb is a nice island, most people come here to settle down and start a family. Making most of the population elderly and parents. This island focuses on farming, because of the rich nutrients in the soil. This island is visited the most during the spring equinox, for its festival. During the festival, people make wooden boats and write their wrongdoings, what they regret, what they're sorry for, and what they want to come in them. Then they put the boat in the water, and light it on fire. The people believe this sends their messages into the universe and frees them.

Smil

Smil is where all the religions lie, the island has 4 of the most important churches. The towns of Smil are each dedicated to a deity, with Beliefs being the interpass of them all. Many people come here on pilgrimages.

Map of Kingdom of Skyward

Skyward is the biggest Kingdom on Sailmoren. Skyward is run by King Halm and King Sal. King Halm spreads his utilities to all of the islands. King Halm tries to keep the peace and keep people safe from Zanyains.

Skyward

This is the capital of The Kingdom of Skyward. In the center of the island is a huge castle. In the castle town, there are all of the representatives of each island. The royal advisors all live in the castle. Skyward is a very beautiful town, especially during the winter season.

May's Island

May's Island is a place of tech, people come here looking to become better thinkers and crafters. May visits the town every once in a while and sponsors a project for the fair in Galm. The fair is where everyone comes up with an invention and brings it to Galm. The king comes down to judge and chose the winner. The winner gets a ticket to live in Skyward, and their project gets reproduced and spread across the world.

Quad 1

Quad 1 is the island used for wood and prison, for most of the island is woodland. Most of Skyward's wood supply comes from Quad 1.

Quad 2

Quad 2 is the manufacturing island. Projects from the fair are sent here to be reproduced. The island holds over 30 factories and supplies jobs to thousands of Skywardians.

Quad 3

Quad 3 is the powering island. This island provides power for all of the Kingdom of Skyward and some of the Kasie's Islands.

Quad 4

Quad 4 is the food island. This island is a fishing and farming island, it provides food for all of the Kingdom of Skyward. For what it can't provide, Fanup helps.

Map of Kingdom of Zanya

Kingdom of Zanya

Not much is known about the Kingdom of Zanya, all we know is that the mountains came from the water to separate the kingdoms. Zanya and Skyward are constantly at war, trying to conquer one another.

Saild

Saild is known for its magic. One of the most magically diverse places in the world. Everything works differently there, stronger magic can create chaos. It is said there is a portal there that can lead to whatever island it wants to. Changing and shifting now and again. Though no one knows where it is exactly, we know it exists.

Map of Unaligned Territories

These Territories don't align with Zanya or Skyward. People seek out these places to be safe, it is said that if you seek asylum, they will grant it.

Mansa

Mansa holds the most beautiful towns, people here live in harmony. Their truffles are to die for. They have some of the nicest people you will ever meet, just don't go starting problems there, you will regret it.

Mountains of Valley

The Mountains of Valley splits the kingdoms of Zanya and Skyward. It houses nomads and peacekeepers, the mountains can be dangerous if you don't have a guide. The monks can change the terrain and stop people from crossing the mountains.
